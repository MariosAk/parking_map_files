# Privacy Policy

## Overview

This privacy policy applies to the **ProThesi app** (the "Application") for mobile devices created by our Service Provider as a Free service.

---

## Information Collection and Use

### Automatically Collected Information

When you download and use the Application, we collect the following information:

- **Internet Protocol (IP) Address** - Your device's IP address
- **Usage Data** - Pages visited, visit times, dates, and time spent on each page
- **App Duration** - Total time spent using the Application
- **Device Information** - Operating system used on your mobile device

### Location Data

The Application collects your device's location to provide enhanced features:

- **Geolocation Services** - Personalized content, relevant recommendations, and location-based services
- **Background Location** - The Application collects location data while running in the background in order to detect when you have left a parking spot and prompt you to notify other drivers. This data is processed on-device and is not stored or transmitted to our servers beyond what is necessary to submit a parking spot report at your explicit confirmation.
- **Analytics & Improvements** - Aggregated and anonymized location data helps us analyze user behavior, identify trends, and improve overall performance
- **Third-Party Services** - We may periodically transmit anonymized location data to external services to enhance the Application and optimize our offerings

### Physical Activity Data

The Application uses your device's physical activity recognition capabilities to detect transitions between walking and driving. This is used solely to determine when you may have vacated a parking spot so the Application can prompt you to share that information with other users.

- Activity data (walking, running, in vehicle) is **processed entirely on-device**
- Activity data is **never transmitted to our servers** or any third party
- This feature can be **disabled at any time** via Settings → Departure Detection

### Information You Provide

For a better experience, we may request certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

- Email address

This information will be used to contact you with important updates, required notices, and marketing promotions.

---

## Background Processing

The Application runs a background service to support parking departure detection. This service:

- Uses your device's location and physical activity data to detect when you leave a parking spot
- Displays a notification asking whether you have vacated a spot — **no data is submitted without your explicit confirmation**
- Can be fully disabled via Settings → Departure Detection
- Stops automatically when you uninstall the Application

## Permissions Used

The Application requests the following device permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION | Show your position on the map and detect nearby parking spots |
| ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION | Detect when you leave a parking spot while the app is in the background |
| ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION | Detect transitions between walking and driving to trigger departure detection |
| FOREGROUND_SERVICE | Keep the departure detection service running reliably |
| POST_NOTIFICATIONS | Send parking spot alerts and departure prompts |

All permissions are used solely for the purposes described above. You may revoke any permission at any time via your device settings, though doing so may limit certain features of the Application.

## User Rights & Data Deletion

### Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all data collection by simply uninstalling the Application using standard uninstall processes available on your mobile device. You may also disable background processing and activity recognition at any time via **Settings → Departure Detection** without uninstalling the app.

### Data Retention & Deletion

**To delete your account and associated personal data:**

1. Open the Application
2. Navigate to **Settings → Delete Account**

**If you cannot access the Application:**

Submit a deletion request via email to: **maappinnovations@gmail.com**

Upon receiving your request, we will delete all personal data (email, last recorded location, etc.). However, certain anonymized data may be retained for statistical or legal purposes.
